About Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, also known as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned scientist, teacher, and the 11th President of India. His contributions to the Indian Space and Missile programs made him a national hero. He inspired millions with his vision for a developed India.

Why We Admire Him

  • His dedication to science and innovation.
  • His humility and love for students.
  • His vision for a developed and self-reliant India.
  • His ability to inspire people to dream big.
